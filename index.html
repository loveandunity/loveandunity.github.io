About us

Quick facts

Diversity

A multicultural and multi-faith lodge uniting men from all walks of life: taxi drivers, doctors, City bankers, software developers, businessmen and everyone in between.

Meetings

The lodge holds four regular meetings each year on 1st Tuesday in February, April, October and December. The meetings are held at Freemasons’ Hall which is located in the heart of London at 60 Great Queen Street, London WC2B 5AZ. The closest tube stations are Holborn and Covent Garden.

Open to new members

If you are interested in joining the lodge, whether already a freemason or not, or are just interested in finding out more about freemasonry, then please use the form below to contact the Secretary who would be delighted to talk further to you.
